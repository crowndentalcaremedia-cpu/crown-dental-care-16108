Frequently Asked Questions About Cosmetic Dentistry

How much do cosmetic dentistry procedures like veneers or teeth whitening cost at Crown Dental Care in Rockville, MD?
At Crown Dental Care, the cost of cosmetic procedures varies based on your needs. For example, professional teeth whitening typically ranges from $350 to $600 per session, while porcelain veneers are usually between $1,200 and $1,500 per tooth. We provide a detailed estimate after your initial consultation, and we’re happy to discuss flexible payment options. Insurance typically does not cover cosmetic treatments, but we can help you understand any available financing.
How long does it take to get cosmetic dental work done, like dental bonding or veneers?
The timeline depends on the specific cosmetic service. Teeth whitening can usually be completed in a single 90-minute appointment. Dental bonding often takes one visit per tooth, usually lasting about an hour. Porcelain veneers generally require two appointments over two to three weeks: one for preparation and impressions, and another for placement. During your consultation at our Rockville office, we’ll create a personalized treatment plan with clear timelines.
Are the dentists at Crown Dental Care licensed and experienced in cosmetic procedures?

We’re committed to providing safe, modern cosmetic dental care to patients in Rockville and surrounding areas.
What is the process for getting a smile makeover at Crown Dental Care?
Your smile makeover starts with a comprehensive consultation where we discuss your goals and evaluate your oral health.
