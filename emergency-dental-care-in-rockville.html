For after-hours emergencies, you can leave a message and our team will reach out first thing the next business day.

The cost of emergency dental care at our Rockville practice depends on your specific needs. An initial emergency exam with necessary X-rays usually ranges from $100 to $200. Are your dentists licensed to handle emergency procedures in Maryland?
Yes, all dentists at Crown Dental Care are fully licensed by the Maryland State Board of Dental Examiners and have extensive experience handling dental emergencies. Our team regularly completes continuing education to stay current on the latest emergency treatment protocols and safety standards.
What is the process for handling a dental emergency at Crown Dental Care?
When you arrive at our Rockville office for an emergency, you'll first complete a brief medical history form, then our team will take any necessary X-rays. One of our dentists will perform a thorough exam, explain your diagnosis, and discuss all treatment options with you. We focus on immediate pain relief and stabilizing your condition, then work with you to create a follow-up care plan if needed. Most emergencies can be treated during your initial visit.
