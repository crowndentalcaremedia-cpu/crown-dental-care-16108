Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Services

How much do common dental procedures like cleanings, fillings, or crowns cost at Crown Dental Care in Rockville?
At Crown Dental Care, prices for common procedures vary based on your specific needs and insurance coverage. As a general guide, routine cleanings start around $120, tooth-colored fillings typically range from $180 to $300 per surface, and crowns usually cost between $1,200 and $1,500. We’re happy to provide a detailed estimate after an initial exam and can help verify your insurance benefits to minimize out-of-pocket costs.
How long does it typically take to get a dental crown from your Rockville office?
The process for getting a crown at our Rockville clinic usually takes two appointments. The first visit involves preparing the tooth, taking digital impressions, and placing a temporary crown—this takes about 90 minutes. Your custom crown is then made in a local dental lab and is ready in about two weeks. At your second appointment (about 30 minutes), we fit and cement your permanent crown. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate busy Montgomery County residents.
Are your dentists licensed and experienced with Maryland-specific dental regulations?
Absolutely. All dentists at Crown Dental Care are fully licensed in Maryland and complete ongoing education to stay current with state regulations and best practices. Our team has extensive experience providing care for patients in Rockville and surrounding areas, ensuring you receive safe, high-quality treatment that meets all state requirements.
What is the process for becoming a new patient at Crown Dental Care, and do you accept walk-ins?
Becoming a new patient is easy! You can call our Rockville office or book online to schedule your first appointment. We’ll ask for your dental and medical history, discuss your concerns, and perform a thorough exam with digital X-rays if needed. While we recommend scheduling ahead, we do our best to accommodate urgent walk-in visits for dental emergencies during business hours.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on dental work performed at your Rockville location?
Yes, we stand behind our work.
